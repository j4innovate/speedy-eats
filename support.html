Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my active order?

Open the Speedy Eats app and tap "My Orders" to see live GPS tracking of your delivery partner.

What if my order is delayed or missing items?

Tap "Help" on your order screen in the app. Our support bot or live team will instantly issue a full refund or re-send missing items.

Is delivery really 100% free on orders over ₹99?

Yes! There are no surge fees, weather fees, or small cart fees on orders over ₹99.
